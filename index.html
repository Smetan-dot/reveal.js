What is JIRA?

  • JIRA Software is a project management, development and communication software developed by Atlassian
  • JIRA is one of the most widely used project management software solutions in the world.
  • According to the 15th Annual State of Agile Report, JIRA is the most highly recommended Agile project management tool in the world

Some statistics

  • Atlassian is one of the 20 biggest software companies on the planet
  • More than 7000 employees working in 13 countries
